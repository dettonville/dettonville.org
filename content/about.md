## About Dettonville

Dettonville is an **open-source-first** initiative focused on building practical, high-quality components for cloud infrastructure.

### Our Approach

We are **not** a consulting company.  
We create focused, independent open-source repositories that solve real problems for platform engineers and cloud architects.

### Goals

- Reduce duplication of infrastructure code across the industry
- Raise the baseline quality of open source cloud components
- Provide battle-tested patterns that scale from startups to large enterprises
- Foster a community of engineers who contribute back real-world solutions

### Current Status

Actively building modules in:
- Terraform & Crossplane
- Kubernetes Platform Tooling
- Observability & Security Baselines
