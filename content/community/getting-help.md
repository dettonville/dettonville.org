You are using Dettonville and ran into a problem?

First check out the [reference guide](/documentation/stable/reference/html/).
If that doesn't help to answer your question you may join the Dettonville [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/dettonville/dettonville/discussions) or hop by the Dettonville [Gitter room](https://gitter.im/dettonville/dettonville-users).
We also monitor the [dettonville](http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/dettonville) tag on StackOverflow.

To report a bug or request a new feature use the Dettonville [issue tracker](https://github.com/dettonville/dettonville/issues). Note that bug reports should be accompanied by a test case demonstrating the issue.
